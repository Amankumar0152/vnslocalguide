О Варанаси / About Varanasi

Varanasi (Варанаси), also known as Benares or Kashi, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world. Situated on the sacred Ganges River, this spiritual capital of India has been a center of learning, culture, and devotion for over 3,000 years.

Experience the mystical evening Ganga Aarti, witness ancient rituals at dawn, explore winding lanes filled with temples, and discover the profound spiritual energy that makes Varanasi truly magical.

🏛️ История: Более 3000 лет культуры
🛶 Ганга: Священная река жизни
🌅 Гхаты: 88 священных ступеней к реке

Must-Visit Attractions / Достопримечательности

🕉️

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

Храм Каши Вишванатх

The most sacred Shiva temple in Varanasi, covered in gold and radiating powerful spiritual energy. A must-visit for every pilgrim and traveler.

🌅

Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh

Ганга Аарти

Witness the mesmerizing evening prayer ceremony with fire, music, and devotion. An unforgettable spiritual spectacle that touches the soul.

🛶

Sunrise Boat Ride

Утренняя прогулка на лодке

Experience the magic of Varanasi at dawn from the Ganges. Watch pilgrims perform rituals as the sun rises over the ancient ghats.

🏛️

Sarnath Buddhist Site

Сарнатх

Where Buddha gave his first sermon. Explore ancient stupas, monasteries, and museums showcasing Buddhist heritage and archaeology.

🎭

Old Varanasi Lanes

Старые улочки Варанаси

Get lost in the maze of ancient alleys filled with temples, silk shops, street food, and authentic local life from centuries past.

🎨

Banarasi Silk Weaving

Ткачество шёлка

Visit traditional silk weaving workshops and witness artisans creating world-famous Banarasi silk sarees with intricate golden patterns.
